Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about purchasing and using our vouchers.

An IT certification voucher is a pre-paid alphanumeric code that you use to pay for your official IT certification exam instead of paying with a credit card at the time of registration.

Generally, our vouchers have a validity of 6 to 12 months from the date of purchase. It is strictly advised to schedule and take your exam before it expires, as validity cannot be extended.

To redeem your voucher, proceed to the official vendor examination site (e.g., Pearson VUE). Select your exam and test center. During checkout, look for an option to "Add Voucher or Promo Code". Enter your specific code there and the balance will drop to zero.

Due to the digital nature of the products, all voucher sales are final. We do not offer refunds or exchanges once the voucher code has been successfully delivered via email unless the code is found to be defective by the vendor.

Vouchers are processed instantly upon successful payment verification. You will receive an email containing your alphanumeric voucher code usually within 10 to 15 minutes.
